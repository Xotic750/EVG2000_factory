Call Feature Settings Help

This page shows the settings for your Call Feature configuration.

Line 1/Line 2/Line 3/Line 4

  • Call Waiting Enable - Choose "yes" to use Call Waiting Service. Call Waiting is that we may have an alternative of talk while the third incoming call towards you happens. Choose "no" to disable.
  • Anonymous Call Rejection Enable - Choose "yes" to use Anonymous Call Rejection Enable Service, and may avoid the disturbance of anonymous incoming call. Choose "no" to disable.
  • Do Not Disturb Enable - Choose "yes" to enable this, and reject the disturbance of all the incoming calls. Choose "no" to disable this.
  • Call Return Enable - Choose "yes" to enable this, and may dial the *69 for dialing back the last incoming call. Choose "no" to disable.
  • CLIR (Caller Line Identification Restriction) Enable - Choose "yes" to enable this, and may hide your outgoing call number. Choose "no" to disable.
  • Call Forwarding Enable - Choose "yes" to enable this, and call forward to another phone under some conditions. Choose "no" to disable.
  • Call Forward Type - Choose Call Forward Service Type here. You can choose one of four types as Call Forwarding condition.
  • Call Forward To - Input the phone number to which you want to call forward.

To Save Or Cancel Changes

Click Apply to have your changes take effect.
Click Cancel to return to the previous unsaved settings.
